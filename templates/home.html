Welcome to Deepfake Detection Project

This project aims to detect fake videos and images using advanced machine learning techniques.

What is Deepfake?

Deepfake technology enables the creation of fake media, including videos and images, that can mislead viewers by replacing real people's faces with computer-generated ones.

How Does Detection Work?

Our machine learning models analyze key features in the video or image to detect inconsistencies and identify deepfakes, providing accurate results in real time.

Why is it Important?

With the rise of deepfakes, it’s crucial to have reliable detection tools to prevent misinformation, protect privacy, and ensure trust in media content.
