About the Project

This project is a collaborative effort to help combat misinformation by detecting deepfake content using advanced machine learning techniques.

Our Mission

We aim to provide tools and resources for detecting deepfake videos and images to prevent the spread of misleading information and ensure the authenticity of media content.

Technology Behind It

Our team utilizes cutting-edge machine learning algorithms to analyze the inconsistencies in visual content, enabling the detection of deepfakes with high accuracy.

Why It Matters

In an age where fake media can be created easily, our goal is to provide a reliable method for detecting deepfakes and protecting the truth in digital content.
